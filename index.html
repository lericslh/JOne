About DARIC

DARIC, developed by J One, is an AI-based system designed to recognize and respond to high-risk situations such as assaults, kidnapping attempts, and general violence. Our mission is to collaborate with governments to enhance urban safety and crime prevention.

Real-Time Monitoring

Advanced AI algorithms analyze video feeds to detect potential threats effectively.

Collaborative Security

Designed to work with government and law enforcement to prevent and address crimes.
